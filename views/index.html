About the researcher

Lalita ‘Lally’ D’Cruze is a final year BSc IT student studying at Blackpool & The Fylde College. Originally from South London she has spent the last 5 years living, studying and working on the Fylde Coast.

About the research

The idea for the dissertation stemmed from the realisation that many users freely provide private data to websites, which appear ‘trustworthy’ through design.

About the questionnaire

The questionnaire should take approximately 5-10 minutes to complete and is made up of three sections; each of which have a slightly different appearance. You are asked to answer a selection of questions in section one and two and in section three you will be asked to evaluate your experience of the first two sections. You will also be asked for your age, gender, location & experience - please be honest and accurate with this information.
